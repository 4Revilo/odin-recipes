Carbonara

Creamy carbonara made easy

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 350 grams of spaghetti
  • 55 grams of grated parmesan
  • 115 grams of baconstrips
  • 2 tbs of cream
  • 1.5 tbs of oil
  • A pinch of salg and pepper

Instructions

  1. Add salt, oil and pasta to boiling water
  2. Mix eggs, cream, salt, pepper and half the cheese in a bowl
  3. Fry the bacon
  4. Mix everthing together and stir well over low heat
