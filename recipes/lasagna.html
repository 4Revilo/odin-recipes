Lasagna

Tasty delicious lasagna topped with cheese and spinach.

Ingredients

  • 450g meat
  • 2 cans of tomatoes
  • Mornay sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pasta sheets
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Thyme

Instructions

  1. Cut the onion, and fry it in a pot
  2. Put the meat in the pot and season it with salt, pepper and thyme
  3. Add the tomatosauce
  4. Let it simmer for an hour
  5. Build the lasagna starting with a sheet of pasta, and adding meatsauce, mornay sauce and cheese
