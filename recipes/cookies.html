Cookies

The best homemade cookies

Ingredients

  • 290 grams of flour
  • 225 grams of melted butter
  • Salt
  • 1 tbs of baking soda
  • 1 tbs of vanilla powder
  • 150 grams of sugar
  • 150 grams of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 grams of dark chokolate

Instructions

  1. Turn on the oven, 190 C
  2. Mix flour, salt and baking soda in a small bowl
  3. Mix butter, sugar and brown sugar in a big bowl
  4. Mix eggs in the big bowl
  5. Mix the small bowl with the big bowl
  6. Mix chopped up chokolate in the dough
  7. Put the dough in the refridgerator for 30 minutes
  8. Put the dough on baking trays and bake for 9-11 minutes
